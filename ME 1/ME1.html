THE BOYZ is a South-Korean boy group with 11 (formerly 12) members, including Q. The video BE YOUR OWN KING was released in order to introduce each member and their unique personalities. It was uploaded on April 14, 2021 through their official channel. The video has 7M+ views.



MAKE YOUR OWN STAGE is Q's version of MAKE YOUR OWN which represents his role as the main dancer of the group. Aside from having dancing skills, he is also a subvocalist.
